# Managing Your Inventory

Everything about your cars is managed from the **admin page** — no files, no
code, no GitHub. Changes go live on the website **in seconds**.

> This replaces the old file-based workflow entirely. If you remember anything
> about "downloading inventory.json" or "pushing to GitHub" — forget it; the
> site now has a real database and photo storage behind `/admin`.

## Signing in

1. Go to **saharmotors.com/admin**
2. Enter the admin password *(ask Hamid if you don't have it)*

## Adding a car

1. Click **Add Vehicle**
2. Fill in the details — only **Make** and **Model** are required
   - Leave **Price** blank to show "Call for Price"
   - Leave **Mileage** blank to show "N/A"
3. **Add photos:** drag them onto the upload box, or tap **browse**
   - **iPhone photos work as-is** — they convert automatically
   - Photos keep the order you selected them in
4. Click **Post — Go Live**. Done — it's on the site.

## Putting photos in the order you want

Every photo tile has **always-visible buttons** underneath:

| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| ‹ / › | Move the photo one spot earlier / later |
| ★ | **Make it the cover photo** (jumps to first) |
| 🗑 | Remove the photo |

You can also **drag any photo onto another spot** to move it there (mouse).
The photo marked **Cover** (always #1) is what shoppers see on the inventory
grid and the homepage.

## Putting cars in the order you want

On the main list, use the **▲ ▼ arrows** at the left of each row. The top of
the list shows **first on the website**. New cars start at the top.

## Editing, duplicating, deleting

- **Edit** — change anything (details or photos), then **Save Changes**
- **Duplicate** — copies a car; edit the copy and post
- **Delete** (trash icon) — removes the car and its photos from the site
- Or set **Status** to *Sold* — hides it from shoppers but keeps your record

## Featured & status

- **Feature on homepage** — checkbox when adding/editing
- **Status:** Available (normal) · Sale Pending (badge) · Sold (hidden from the main list)

## Backup

Click **Backup** anytime to download your whole inventory as a file.

---

**Troubleshooting**

- *"Uploading N photos…" seems stuck* — each photo gets up to a minute before it
  reports an error; iPhone photos take a few seconds each to convert. If one
  fails you'll see exactly which file and why.
- *A photo shows "Photo coming soon"* — it's still propagating; give it a few
  seconds and refresh.
- *Password not working* — ask Hamid to reset it (10-second change, no redeploy).
